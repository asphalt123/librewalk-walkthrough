# Great Mollusks, Great Remedies

## Infiltration and Recruitment
- Leave **Helen** and head to the **Port of Piraeus**.
- Eliminate degenerate hosts along the way and enter the **SLUG warehouse**.
- Convince **Sedrick** to join your side.

## Component Collection
- Retrieve the **Sprat essence** from the team leader's office.
- Go to the entrance of the **Pierced Caves**, north of the wild wastes.
- Head southwest and open the chest to obtain the **parasite egg pods**.

## Synthesis and Conclusion
- Return to your **hotel suite** and use the **centrifuge** to formulate the **antiparasitic**.
- Head to the **Timonerie Museum**.
- Meet **PHAETON** upstairs and disperse the treatment to complete the quest.
